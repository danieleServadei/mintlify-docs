---
title: 'Quickstart'
description: 'Your first steps with the AI Fraud Detector.'
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Account">
  After receiving the [invitation](/essentials/invitations) link to an organization, you can create your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign in">
  Sign in to your account using the username and password you provided during account creation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request your Api Keys">
  Generate your [API key](/essentials/api-keys) from the API keys page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your webhook url">
  In the organization settings page, set the [webhook url](/essentials/webhooks) that will call after the validation of the entities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the validation criteria">
  Verify that the [validation criteria](/essentials/criteria) are configured (there must be at least 3) and suitable for your organization. You can do this from the organization settings page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage the auto-validation">
  [Automate](/essentials/auto-validation) the validation of the entities to speed up the process. You can do this from the organization settings page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Insert Entities">
  Insert the entities you want to detect through the [API](/api-reference/endpoint/insert).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Validate Entities">
  Accept or Refuse the AI's assessment or modify a validation already made.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the webhook call status">
  After 5 minutes of the validation of the entity, the [webhook](/essentials/webhooks) will be called. You can check the status of the call in the webhook page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite new users to yout organization">
  Expand your organization by [inviting](/essentials/invitations) new people from the invitations page."
  </Step>
</Steps>
